In this example:

<h1>, <h2>, <h3>, <h4>, <h5>, and <h6> are used for headings with different levels of importance.
<p> is used for paragraphs of text.
<strong> is used for bold text, <em> is used for italic text, and <u> is used for underlined text.
<br> is used for line breaks.
<img> is used to display an image.
<video> is used to embed a video with controls.
<table>, <tr>, <th>, and <td> are used to create a simple table structure.
The <title> tag sets the title of the HTML document.
